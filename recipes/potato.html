Holiday-Only Mashed Potatoes

Description

Mashed potatoes enhanced with heavy cream. They may be high in fat, but it's the holidays for a reason!

All of your guests will be asking you what you did to make this mouth watering dish so rich

Ingredients:

  • 5 pounds red potatoes, peeled and quartered
  • 1/2 cup butter (unsalted)
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • salt and pepper to taste

Steps:

  1. Place potatoes into a large pot with water to cover; bring to a boil and cook until fork tender, approx. 20 minutes. Drain
  2. Add butter to potatoes in pot. Mash using a potato masher, ricer, or mixer until smooth. Gradually beat in heavy cream until well combined. Season with salt and pepper.
  3. Serve hot and enjoy!
