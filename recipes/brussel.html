Maple Roasted Brussel Sprouts with Bacon

Description

These Brussel Sprouts are paired with crumbled bacon and are roasted in a mixture of maple syrup and olive oil.

The roasting process gives the dish a crispy texture and offers a perfect sweet yet savory side to complement your dinner!

Ingredients:

  • Brussel Sprouts
  • Bacon
  • Salt and Pepper
  • Olive Oil
  • Maple Syrup

Steps:

  1. Gather all ingredients. Preheat the oven to 400 degrees F
  2. Line a rimmed baking sheet with aluminum foil
  3. Trim ends off Brussel Sprouts and cut any large pieces in half
  4. Transfer to a large bowl and add bacon, salt, and pepper
  5. Drizzle Maple Syrup and Olive Oil making sure to coat evenly
  6. Transfer to prepared baking sheet and place in preheated oven
  7. Dish should take approximately 20 to 30 minutes until bacon is crispy and brussel sprouts are caramelized - stir halfway through
  8. Serve warm and enjoy!
